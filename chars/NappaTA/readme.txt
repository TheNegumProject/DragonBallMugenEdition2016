===========================
Nappa by The Answer
===========================
I made Nappa in a custom 6 button style I hope you enjoy.


Controls
========

System
--------------
x+a - kof dodge
y+b - powercharge
z+c - powerdash cancel (500 power)

Specials
---------------
QCF + P - Flame Pillar, (otg)
QCF + PP - EX Flame Pillar (otg, 500 power)
DP + P - Eye Beam
DP + PP - EX Eye Beam (500 power)
QCF + K - Power Tackle
QCF + KK - EX Power Tackle (500 power)

Supers
-------------
QCB + P - Super Counter (1000 power)
QCF,QCF + P - Bomber DX (1000 power, Air OK)
QCB,QCB + P - Kapa (1000 power)
QCF,QCF + K - Arm Crash (1000 power)
QCB,QCB + K - Kapa lv2 (2000 power)
QCB,F + HP - Genocide Sphere (2000 power)
QCF,QCF + PP - Giant Storm (3000 power)
