2) Fury Bar :

Every character has a special bar, which divides in two.

Fury level: This yellow bar grows up when the character's attack hits the opponent (only the special and normal moves), and when the character is hurt. When this bar is full, you can use the Hyper Fury. (A big Z appear)

Z-Assist Time : Every time the character uses his Z-Assist, it resets and the character must wait few seconds until the bar recharges.

 

3) Specials Features :

 

Launcher: Crouching Close Punch or Crouching Close Kick allows you to send the opponent in the air and follow up with a super jump.

Z-Assist : At the start of the match you have to choose between 3 Assists : Oozaru, Saibaiman or Turles. Then during the fight you could call the assist by pressing (Don't forget you need power > 500 and wait 9 seconds to use again).

WinResult: When you win a match, after the winpose, a special screen appear. It shows you how you fight in 3 categories : Life, Time and Attack.
