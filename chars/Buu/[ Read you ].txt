|___________________________________[    System    ]___________________________________|

A  - Infinite Attack
   +D Infinite Ender
   +F Infinite Ender 2nd (-500 power)
   +U Pursuit

A  + Forward - Infinite Scramble (-1000 power as a chain)
   -> Hold A to charge 
   -> Can be performed after normal attacks

A  + Down    - Ki Blast       -250 power

              * Can be performed after grounded normals

FF | BB       - DASH (In Air OK) 
D -> U        - Super Jump
